Overview of the development stages in Microsoft Power Platform

As a Microsoft Gold Partner equipped with certified solution experts, we provide efficient Power Platform development services tailored to enhance or create your business applications. Leveraging a deep understanding of the Power Platform and its functionalities, our specialists aid in identifying and fulfilling business requirements using the appropriate blend of technology and methodologies

Our team adheres to a systematic process when constructing applications for businesses, encompassing the following phases:

Discovery

  • Identifying the problem or challenge
  • Gathering requirements
  • Defines scope of the project
01

Design

  • Power Platform solutions layout
  • Designing solution components and architecture
  • Creation of solution blueprint
02

Development

  • Build solutions components based on design layout
  • Implement custom coding logic
  • Integration with external systems
03

Testing

  • Functionality testing
  • Integration testing
  • User acceptance testing
04

Deployment

  • Deploying to production environment
  • Configuring settings and migrating data
